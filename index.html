WG VISITOR PARKING 5357-04 RULES AND REGULATIONS

(Updated 5/23/25. Scroll down to register your vehicle by text message.)

  • To ensure fair and equal opportunity for all residents and their guests, permitted PARKING TIME IS LIMITED UPTO 24 HOURS per each QR code scan. After 24 hours expire or upon changing spots, a new scan and registration are required.
  • Overstay parking (no scan within the grace period) will be charged $30, and the vehicle will be subject to towing.
  • Each condo unit receives 8 x 24-hour shared free parking credits per calendar month. Credits are not transferable to the next month or to a different unit.
  • Excess parking by any/all vehicles associated with a condo unit will be charged at $15 per day. A scan is required for each day.
  • Vehicles parked without QR code scan and text message registration will be fined, tagged, and subject to towing.
  • The Association reserves the right to use all legal means of immobilizing or bracing the vehicle while on the Association's property until all fees are collected in full.
  • Vehicle operators must agree to all rules above and register their vehicle via the button below by sending a text message with the required information.
  • If you do not agree or fail to send the required registration text, you must remove your vehicle or it will be removed at the owner's expense.
  • Thank you for your cooperation!
