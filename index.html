WG VISITOR PARKING 5357-04 RULES AND REGULATIONS

(Updated 6/1/25. Scroll down to register your vehicle by text message.)

  • To ensure fair use and equal opportunity for all residents and their guests, PARKING TIME IS LIMITED UPTO 24 HOURS per each QR code scan. After 24 hours expire or upon changing spots, a new scan and registration are required.
  • Overstay parking (no scan within 20 min grace period) will be fined $30, and the vehicle might be subject to towing.
  • Each home receives 8 x 24-hour shared free parking credits per calendar month. Credits are not transferable to the next month or to a different unit.
  • After free time is used up, excess parking by any/all vehicles associated with a condo unit will be charged at $15 per day. A scan is required for each day.
  • Vehicles parked without QR code scan and text message registration will be fined $50, tagged, and subject to towing.
  • The Association reserves the right to use all legal means of citation, immobilizing/ bracing and towing of a vehicle while on the Association's property to enforce these parking rules.
  • Condo Owners are responsible for fines and parking fees of their tennants and their guests.
  • Vehicle operators must agree to all rules above and register their vehicle via the button below by sending a text message with the required information.
  • If you do not agree or fail to send the required registration text, you must remove your vehicle without delay or it will be removed at the owner's expense.
  • Thank you for your cooperation!
